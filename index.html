Frequently asked questions

Need More help? hello@theneostudio.com

What services do you offer? +

We provide end-to-end interior design solutions including schematic design, 3D visualizations, space planning, material selection, furniture specification, and full project management from conception to completion.

Do you handle both residential and commercial projects? +

Yes, we specialize in both residential homes and commercial spaces, tailoring our design approach to meet the specific functional and aesthetic needs of each environment.

How long does a typical project take? +

Timelines vary based on scope. Discovery usually takes 2-4 weeks, design development 6-12 weeks, and installation can range from 4-16 weeks depending on material lead times and site complexity.

Can you work with my existing furniture? +

Absolutely! We offer space planning and reconfiguration services that can integrate your existing pieces into a new, cohesive design vision while supplementing with new specifications where needed.

How can I start a project with you? +

Starting is easy! Simply fill out the contact form above or reach out via email. We'll begin with a consultation to understand your vision, lifestyle, and space requirements.
